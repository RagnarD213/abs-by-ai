Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Abs by AI ("we", "our") provides an AI-powered fitness visualization service at absbyai.com and through the Abs by AI Android app. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

Photos you upload

  • Photos are sent over an encrypted connection (HTTPS) to our server, which forwards them to AI providers — Anthropic (Claude) for photo review and Google (Gemini) for image generation — to produce your transformation image.
  • We do not store your photos or your generated images on our servers. They are processed in memory and returned to your device.
  • AI providers process images under their own terms: Anthropic Privacy Policy and Google Privacy Policy.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details.

Analytics

We use PostHog to collect anonymized usage analytics (pages visited, buttons clicked, approximate region) so we can improve the product. We do not sell your data.

Age requirement

Abs by AI is intended for adults. You must be 18 or older to use the service. Photos that appear to depict minors are automatically rejected.

Data retention

We keep no user accounts and no photo library. Server logs (IP address, request time) are retained briefly for security and rate limiting, then discarded.

Your choices

Because we don't store your photos or personal profiles, there is typically nothing to delete. For any privacy question or request, contact us and we'll respond promptly.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@absbyai.com
